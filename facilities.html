The Campus

Everything in One Place

The Shekhinah campus sits on 5 acres of land strategically located on National Highway 44, north of Hyderabad. It is thoughtfully designed so that all amenities are within easy walking distance of each other.

  • Main Auditorium (615 seats)
  • A/C Mini Hall (80 seats)
  • Non-A/C Mini Hall (250 seats)
  • Open Hall (300 seats)
  • Girls' Accommodation Block
  • Boys' Accommodation Block
  • Dining Hall (350 seats)
  • Athletics Track & Sports Field
  • Mini Supermarket
  • Generator Room
🗺️
On-Campus Amenities

Everything Your Group Needs

🏃

Athletics Track

A full athletics track for running, relays and warm-up activities — perfect for youth camps that need physical activity built into the programme.

⚽

Sports Field

Spacious sports field for football, cricket, team-building games and outdoor activities. A huge hit with youth groups.

🙏

Prayer Areas

Quiet, designated areas on campus for individual and group prayer — an essential part of the retreat experience.

🛒

Mini Supermarket

A small supermarket on campus stocked with snacks, drinks and basic necessities so guests never have to leave the campus for essentials.

⚡

Generator Backup

A powerful Kirloskar generator ensures 24/7 uninterrupted power — no disruptions to your event, ever, regardless of grid failures.

🌿

Green Open Spaces

Beautiful greenery and open lawns ideal for informal fellowship, quiet time, outdoor games and creating that retreat atmosphere your group came for.

🚗

Ample Parking

Generous parking space for buses, vans and cars — no stress about parking when your group arrives by their own transport.

🔒

Safe, Gated Campus

A secure, gated campus with proper supervision — giving parents and leaders peace of mind, especially for youth groups and school events.

📍

Strategic Location

On National Highway 44, just 1 hour from central Hyderabad. Easy access by road, and close to Wadiaram railway station (5 km).

⚽
For Youth Camps

Sports, Space and Energy

Youth groups love Shekhinah — not just for the sessions in the auditorium, but for everything outside too. A full athletics track, sports field and open green spaces give young people room to run, compete and build friendships.

  • Full athletics running track
  • Sports field for football, cricket and more
  • Open grounds for games and activities
  • Open Hall ideal for evening sessions and worship nights
  • Dormitory accommodation for large youth groups
  • On-campus supermarket for snacks and drinks
