Common Questions

Accommodation FAQs

Are the washrooms shared or attached?

Every room in each accommodation unit has its own attached washroom — no shared bathrooms.

Is there separate accommodation for girls and boys?

Yes. The campus has completely separate accommodation blocks for girls and boys, with appropriate supervision.

Can we mix accommodation types?

Absolutely. You can mix executive rooms for leaders and dormitories for the larger group — we'll work out the best arrangement for you.

Are meals included in accommodation?

Meals can be added as a package. We offer in-house catering for all three meals. See our Dining page for details.

What is the maximum capacity for accommodation?

The campus can accommodate over 350 guests in total across all room types.
