Shekhinah Main Auditorium

The flagship venue — a full-scale convention hall that rivals the best in the region, built for large conferences, crusades, youth gatherings and church events of all kinds.

✅ Theatre-style seating
❄️ Fully air-conditioned
🎭 Balcony seating
🎵 Professional sound system
💡 Stage lighting rig
⚡ Generator backup

Shekhinah Open Hall

Set amidst the greenery of our 5-acre campus, this covered open-air hall offers a serene, naturally ventilated space for gatherings that benefit from an outdoor feel.

🌿 Amidst greenery
🌬️ Natural ventilation & shade
🎭 Stage included
🔊 Sound/projection ready

The covered roof provides shade and protection while the open sides allow a cool breeze throughout. This venue is particularly popular for evening worship sessions, camp fires and relaxed fellowship meetings.
